Safe Dating Tips

Protect Your Personal Information

- Do not share your home address, workplace address, or daily routine with someone you have not met in person.
- Avoid sharing financial information, including bank details, credit card numbers, or your Social Security number.
- Use the in-app messaging feature to communicate. Avoid moving to personal phone numbers, email, or other platforms too quickly.
- Be cautious about sharing your full name, social media handles, or other identifying details early on.

Take Your Time

- Get to know someone through the app before agreeing to meet in person.
- Pay attention to how they communicate. Watch for inconsistencies in their stories or details.
- Trust your instincts. If something feels off, it probably is.
- Do not feel pressured to meet before you are ready. A respectful person will understand.

Meet in Public

- Always choose a public, well-lit location for your first few dates — coffee shops, restaurants, parks, or other busy venues.
- Avoid meeting at your home or your date's home until you feel completely comfortable and have built trust.
- Consider choosing a place you are familiar with so you feel more at ease.

Tell Someone Your Plans

- Let a friend or family member know where you are going, who you are meeting, and when you expect to be back.
- Share your live location with a trusted contact using your phone's location-sharing feature.
- Arrange to check in with someone during or after the date.
