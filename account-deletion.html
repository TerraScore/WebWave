Account & Data Deletion

Last Updated: May 13, 2026
‍
App: Wavy
Developer: Wavy Dating
‍
This page explains how to permanently delete your Wavy account and the personal data associated with it.
‍
1. Delete Your Account From Inside the App
The fastest way to delete your account is from within the Wavy app:
1. Open the Wavy app and sign in.
2. Tap your Profile icon.
3. Tap Settings.
4. Tap Account.
5. Tap Delete Account and confirm.
Your account is queued for deletion immediately and removed from our active systems within 30 days.
‍
2. Request Deletion by Email
If you cannot access the app, you can request account and data deletion by email. Send a message from the email address associated with your account (or include the phone number you signed up with) to:
‍
Email: support@wavydating.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request
‍
Please include:
- The phone number associated with your Wavy account.
- Your registered name (if known).
- Confirmation that you are the account holder and want your account and personal data permanently deleted.
‍
We will verify your identity and process the request within 30 days.
‍
3. What Data Is Deleted
When your deletion request is processed, the following data is permanently removed from our active systems:
- Your profile (name, date of birth, gender, bio, preferences, interests).
- Profile photos and any uploaded media.
- Phone number and account credentials.
- Matches, likes, swipes, and chat history.
- Location data and device identifiers tied to your account.
- Subscription, push notification, and verification records tied to your account.
‍
4. What Data May Be Retained, and Why
For legal, safety, fraud-prevention, and dispute-resolution reasons, a limited set of records may be retained for a maximum of 12 months after deletion, in aggregated or restricted form:
- Records related to fraud, abuse, safety reports, or violations of our Terms & Conditions.
- Transaction and billing records required by tax and accounting law.
- Anonymized, non-identifying analytics that cannot be linked back to you.
- Information we are legally required to retain, or that is needed to comply with law-enforcement requests.
‍
After this retention window, residual records are deleted or permanently anonymized.
‍
5. Restoring a Deleted Account
Account deletion is permanent. Once your account is deleted, profile content, matches, and chat history cannot be restored. You may sign up again as a new user using the same or a different phone number.
‍
6. Contact
If you have any questions about deleting your account or the handling of your data, contact us at: support@wavydating.com
